Features that make us unique

Our Vision Express increases clinical efficiency and staff productivity, truly powering every part of your practice.

Optical Point-of-Sale

We make optical sales a natural extension of the eye care experience. Process sales with speed, accuracy, and efficiency. The Quick Order feature is the ideal point-of-sale (POS) and a convenient way to create orders, fill prescriptions, add payments, and more — all on one screen. Inventory management allows you to maintain accurate control of frames, contact lenses, spectacle lenses, lens treatments, services, and other items for a single practice or multiple locations. Look-up and transfer items across locations and easily keep track of inventory in real-time.

Electronic Health Records

Our MACRA-ready, EHR empowers you to fully document eye exams with speed and ease. Perform paperless eye examinations efficiently and also reduce errors. The 2015 edition certified Health IT Module is fully integrated into a complete electronic record-keeping system that helps your practice every step of the way.

Patient Engagement

Intensive Care

Practice Management
